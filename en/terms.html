Terms & Conditions.

Last updated: 14 May 2026. These General Terms and Conditions (GTC) govern all software development, consulting, and operational service contracts between Vision GO GmbH and our business clients. The German-language version (AGB) is legally binding.

1. Scope of application

These GTC apply to all contracts concluded between Vision GO GmbH (hereinafter "Vision GO") and entrepreneurs within the meaning of §1 KSchG (B2B only). Deviating terms of the client require our written consent. Consumer transactions (B2C) are not within the scope of these GTC.

2. Conclusion of contract

Our offers are non-binding until acceptance. A contract is concluded when Vision GO confirms a signed offer or statement of work (SoW) in writing. E-mail confirmation suffices for written form.

3. Scope of services

3.1 Discovery & architecture

Initial phase: requirement analysis, technical architecture, risk assessment, and a written estimate. Discovery is typically billed time-and-material at the daily rate stated in the SoW.

3.2 Development

Implementation phase: agile sprints of two weeks, weekly status calls, fortnightly demos. Source code is delivered via a Git repository hosted by Vision GO or, at the client's option, by the client themselves.

3.3 Operations & SLAs

Optional post-launch operations: hosting, monitoring, incident response, on-call. Service levels (response times, uptime targets) are governed by a separate SLA appendix to the main contract.

4. Client obligations to cooperate

The client undertakes to:

  • Provide all information, materials and access (credentials, design files, third-party APIs) required for performance in a timely manner.
  • Nominate one decision-making contact person and one technical contact person.
  • Respond to feedback requests, design reviews and approval requests within five business days. Delays in response shift agreed deadlines accordingly.
  • Provide test data and a testing environment for acceptance tests.

5. Fees & payment terms

Unless otherwise stipulated in the SoW:

  • Time-and-material engagements are billed monthly in arrears at the agreed daily rate (currently € 1,100 – 1,400 net depending on seniority).
  • Fixed-price engagements are billed in three milestones: 30% on contract signing, 40% on first demo, 30% on acceptance.
  • Travel and accommodation outside Vienna are invoiced separately at cost.
  • All prices are net of VAT. Vision GO's VAT-ID is ATU79520014.
  • Invoices are due 14 days net. Default interest of 9.2 percentage points above the EU base rate applies (§456 UGB).

6. Acceptance

Deliverables are deemed accepted when the client signs an acceptance protocol or uses the deliverable in production for more than 14 days without written objection. Minor defects do not entitle the client to refuse acceptance.

7. Rights of use & intellectual property

Upon full payment, the client receives an exclusive, perpetual, worldwide right to use the software developed specifically for them (work-for-hire). Vision GO retains the right to use generic libraries, frameworks, and patterns we developed before or independently of the engagement. Open-source components remain governed by their respective licenses.

8. Warranty & liability

Warranty period is 24 months from acceptance. During this period, Vision GO will remedy defects free of charge that are not caused by improper use, third-party intervention, or environmental changes outside our control.

Liability is limited to typical, foreseeable damages and capped at the total fee paid by the client in the last 12 months prior to the damaging event. Liability for slight negligence is excluded. Liability for personal injury and damages caused by intent or gross negligence remains unaffected.

9. Data protection & confidentiality

Vision GO and the client mutually undertake to maintain confidentiality regarding business and trade secrets disclosed during the engagement, also after termination of the contract. Where Vision GO processes personal data on behalf of the client, the parties shall conclude a separate data processing agreement (DPA) pursuant to Art. 28 GDPR.

10. References & marketing

Vision GO is entitled to name the client by name and logo on its website, in pitch decks and on social media, and to describe the project in general terms. The client may object to this in writing at any time. Confidential details (financials, technical internals) are never disclosed.

11. Termination

Open-ended retainer agreements may be terminated by either party with three months' notice to the end of a calendar month. Fixed-term contracts can be terminated by either party for good cause without notice (e.g. material breach, insolvency). Services rendered up to termination are invoiced in full.

12. Final provisions

Place of performance is Vienna. The law of the Republic of Austria applies, excluding the UN Convention on Contracts for the International Sale of Goods (CISG) and the conflict-of-law rules. The competent court for all disputes arising from the contract is the court with subject-matter jurisdiction for the 1st district of Vienna. Should individual provisions of these GTC be or become invalid, the validity of the remaining provisions remains unaffected.
