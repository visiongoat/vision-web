Privacy Policy.

Last updated: 14 May 2026. This notice explains what personal data Vision GO GmbH processes when you visit our website, contact us, or use our apps — and how you can exercise your rights under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Introduction

We take the protection of your personal data seriously. We process personal data only in accordance with the GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) and the Austrian Data Protection Act (DSG). The German-language version of this notice is the legally binding one — see Datenschutzerklärung.

Vision GO has not appointed an external Data Protection Officer (none required under Art. 37 GDPR). For any privacy matter please contact info@visiongo.at.

3. Collection and storage of personal data

3.1 Automatic data collection when visiting the website

When you visit visiongo.at, our hosting provider automatically collects the following data, which is technically required to deliver the page:

  • IP address (anonymized after 7 days)
  • Date and time of the request
  • Page requested (URL)
  • Browser type and version, operating system
  • Referrer URL (if available)

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR — legitimate interest in operating a secure, functional website. Storage period: 30 days in server log files, then automatic deletion.

3.2 Contact form or e-mail

If you contact us via our contact form or by e-mail, we process the data you provide voluntarily (name, e-mail address, company, message) for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR (steps prior to entering into a contract) or Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to inquiries). Storage period: until the inquiry has been handled, then deletion within 6 months unless a contract follows.

4. Cookies and analytics tools

4.1 Cookies

We only use one technically necessary local storage entry (vg_consent_v2) to remember whether you have already seen the cookie notice. This is not transmitted to any third party. No marketing or tracking cookies are set.

4.2 Analytics tools

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, Hotjar or comparable third-party analytics tools. We do not build user profiles, do not perform cross-site tracking, and do not run retargeting. We may aggregate anonymized server-log metrics (e.g. page views, error rates) for capacity planning.

5. Fonts & external resources

This website loads Inter, JetBrains Mono and Fraunces from Google Fonts. Your browser briefly contacts fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com. We are evaluating self-hosting these fonts in a future update to avoid the connection altogether. Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR — legitimate interest in visually consistent presentation.

6. Hosting

This website is hosted on infrastructure operated by Hetzner Online GmbH (Industriestr. 25, 91710 Gunzenhausen, Germany) and Cloudflare, Inc. (CDN, EU edge nodes). Data is processed exclusively in the EU. Order processing agreements (Art. 28 GDPR) are in place with both providers.

7. Disclosure of your data

Your data will not be sold or rented to third parties. We disclose data only:

  • To processors (hosting, e-mail provider) bound by Art. 28 GDPR contracts.
  • To authorities, where a statutory obligation exists (e.g. tax records, criminal investigations).
  • With your explicit prior consent.

8. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — to know which data we process.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — to have your data deleted.
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18) — to limit processing.
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20) — to receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Austrian Data Protection Authority (dsb.gv.at).

To exercise any of these rights, e-mail info@visiongo.at. We respond within 30 days at the latest.

9. Data security

We use HTTPS (TLS 1.3) for all data transmission. Access to internal systems is protected by strong passwords, two-factor authentication, and least-privilege access controls. Backups are encrypted with AES-256 and stored on EU servers.

10. Data processing for clients

When we develop software on behalf of a client and that software processes personal data (e.g. end-user logins, payment data), Vision GO acts as a processor under Art. 28 GDPR. Our standard order-processing agreement is available on request to info@visiongo.at.

11. Vision GO apps

Our apps — MegaRadio, ScanUp, eSIMfo, TaxiHub, Online Snake — each ship with their own privacy policy in the app stores. The general principles described here apply (no third-party tracking SDKs, EU-only storage, GDPR-compliant deletion on request).

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may amend this notice to reflect changed legal requirements or changes to our services. The current version is always available at visiongo.at/en/privacy.html and was last updated on 14 May 2026.
